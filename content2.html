Network Installation services

At Hightech, we value a job done right.We are based in LA and provide services throughout the Western part of the United States. With over 100 years combined experience, we provide the necessary skilled labor to install your entire network cabling requirements. We are not an agency. We are experienced and qualified for the installation, termination and testing of Cat6, Cat5E, Voice/Data and Fiber Optic Cabling. We are skilled in the installation of cable containment and can provide an A/V Installation and Commissioning Service. We can provide specialist on site supervision for new installs, refurbishments, moves, additions and changes (MAC). We can also carryout communications room clean ups, audits, re-patching and patching requests. From very large, multiple outlet cable infrastructures to small bespoke needs it is our aim to provide a flexible and Warranty Systems that you can trust. We can provide labor on a day rate or Bid.
