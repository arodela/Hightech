About CEO Julian

As president of C & P Installations, Chris has over 25 years of experience in the material-handling field. He started in 1972 as an installer working in the family business. "Without a high quality installer, even the best product is incomplete," Chris notes. Chris founded C & P Installations with the goal of providing his customers with top quality installations. Chris remains active in all aspects of the day to day business, providing job quotes and consulting on the selection of the most appropriate product.
